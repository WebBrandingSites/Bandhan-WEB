Company Overview

Bandhan Contracting & General Maintenance LLC

Bandhan Contracting & General Maintenance LLC was established in the UAE to meet the growing demand for specialized services. Our operations span the globe and encompass diverse sectors within the industry, covering technical, civil, piping, plumbing, aluminum, metal, and general maintenance works for businesses and residents alike.

With over four decades of combined experience, our leadership and technical teams have proven success across various industries. We bring that wealth of knowledge to every project, ensuring tailored end-to-end solutions crafted specifically for you.

Our Vision

To become one of the most reliable and leading organizations in the contracting and general maintenance sector within the UAE.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional value to our customers through delivering highest quality technical solutions, on-time project completion, and outstanding customer service.
